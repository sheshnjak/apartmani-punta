About us

Tomislav and Božena Juričin are young couple with experience in tourism. They fluently speak English and German, and can communicate in Italian and Czech.

They like flexible and non-intrusive approach to their guests and they leave guests peacefully enjoy they vacation, but they are always available for any kind of service or advice.

 

Contact and reservation

The easiest way to make a reservation for upcoming season is to fill a form on the bottom of this page, or send an e-mail to .

If you send e-mail, it should contain information on desired date of your arrival and number of persons involved. After that we send you an e-mail with our official bidding. All our accommodation periods last seven days, and to ensure your booking our bid will contain 30% advance payment. By that advanced payment you guarantee your arrival and oblige us to ensure your reserved accommodation.

Besides accommodation, we offer our guests genuine home-made Dalmatian olive oil, brandy, wine and the like products. Also, it is possible to arrange that we prepare you a traditional dinner with grill fish or some other domestic food.

Of course, you can freely use our fireplace at any time and prepare your own grills, depending on your wishes. All our apartments have own terrace that is separated from others by hedge, so you will have your quietness and privacy. 

Other informations

All our guests have enough parking space, and if you have your own boat we can arrange a berth for you. Your pets are also welcome at our house. Our single room apartments are in the basement, so we can adjust them to ease access for people with disabilities if it is needed. Punta beach is only 50 meters away, and there's a promenade about 500 meters long to the town center.
