Apartments

We are offering accommodation in standard apartments type Studio. All the apartments have recently been rebuilt and refurnished. There are two single room apartments A3+2 (3 or 5 person) at the basement and one detached room S2+1 (2 person). Also, on the first floor there is one two room apartment A4+2 (4 or 6 person). Since single room apartments and detached room are in the basement and they can easily be adapted to enable access for people with disabilities.

Pricing is listed for every apartment (or room) in respective table, depending on part of season. Residence tax is not included in price. For July and August is 1,00 EUR per person per day. For other months is 0,80 EUR per person per day. Residence permit for children under 12 years is free of charge. Residence permit for children between 12 and 18 years only 50% of the price. Supplement for a pet/day 4 EUR.
 

Our house is situated in western part of town called Punta, and there is only 50 meters walk to the beach. Our yard is big enough to ensure parking places for all our guests.
